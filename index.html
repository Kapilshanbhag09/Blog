Suruchi is a restaurant that has been operating in Belgaum for over 2 years and has now branched out , with authentic Indian cuisine that takes you on a grand and passionate journey of flavours. The most popular dish prepared is the local Belgaum biryani, which gives you a taste of heritage that is unique to the city, with a strong aroma of saffron in Belgaum Basmati Rice and delectable chunks of succulent meat cooked to perfection, with the blending of tradition spices and vegetables creating a dish that is refined, delicious and subtly flavored. The biryani is usually enjoyed with dahi chutney to enhance the taste of a delectable, warm and fragrant meal with a heavenly twist to this favourite Indian delicacy.

The restaurant also serves mouthwatering Awadhi delicacies, kebabs and a variety of other dishes prepared using the freshest ingredients handpicked by highly qualified chefs who work in unison to bring you a culinary experience par excellence, ensuring that guests have a delightful dining experience with excellent service and an ambience best suited for a leisurely lunch or dinner with family.
